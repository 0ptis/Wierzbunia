Main page Moons Enemies

Your main objective while working for the company is to retrieve all the valuable objects you may find upon the moons you shall be exploring. The more profit you bring to the company, the more rewards you shall receive in the later stages of your exploration. The excess money from the quota you meet will be at your will to spend on item that may contribute in order to ease your time out of the ship. Of course as you progres and earn more resources the goals we as the company will set for you will progresivley get more demanding.
